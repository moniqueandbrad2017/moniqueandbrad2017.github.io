Monique and Brad worked very dutifully together and enjoyed attending yoga class in each other's company and running statistical experiments on video games. Once during yoga, Monqiue and Brad accidentally touched feet during three-legged dog and the rest is history. Also, Monqiue had one too many at a holiday party and - the poor guy - Brad had no chance. Monique had her eyes on the prize and the rest was REALLY history.

Staying in PDX

Airbnbs

There are boatloads of Airbnbs in the area. Some suggested areas are Kerns which is right by the venue and the Mississippi District . Those links will get you directly to Airbnb searches. If you are new to Airbnb, sign up through this link to get $30 off your first booking.

There are also hotels aplenty if you want to stab a knife right though Brad's warm beating heart.

Getting Around

Portland is alternative transportation friendly. Our public transit is called Max and you can pick it up right at the airport and use it to get around town.

There is a new bikeshare service called Biketown use to ride bikes one-way and is super easy and fun. There is also Lyft/Uber, which if you haven't used here is alink to get $50 in free rides.
